Things To Do

A charming beachside fishing village on the central Pacific coast, Esterillos Oeste is an ideal destination for travelers wanting to experience the pura vida for which Ticos are known. Endless, no crowds, incredible sunsets, and some of the top surf in Costa Rica best describe the volcanic beach of Esterillos. Swaying palms and almond trees line the edge and the water is warm. At low tide, the size of the beach increases dramatically, creating clear natural pools and sand waiting to be explored. The neighbourhood has a small grocery and liquor store, as well as a few restaurants and bars. Besides surfing and swimming, activities such as fishing, a variety of eco-tours, zip lining in the forest canopy, horseback riding, and more can be easily arranged.
