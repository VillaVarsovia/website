The Villa

Villa Varsovia is a modern interpretation of a traditional open-air Spanish villa with a very private interior courtyard that includes a 16'x32' pool and rancho. Polish owners, Margaret and Andrew Wieladek, who permanently reside in Canada, built the villa in 2012. Standing alone on a breezy hill, this vacation rental is a secluded oasis surrounded by lush jungle. Banana and mango trees grow in the garden; playful monkeys are regular visitors; curious iguanas live on the grounds; and scarlet macaws fly above in pairs, squawking like an old married couple. The villa is on the central Pacific coast and within walking distance of the endless beach of Esterillos. Off the beaten path, it is located between the bustling town of Jaco and world famous Manuel Antonio National Park.

Location

An easy drive of approximately 90 minutes from San Jose airport, Villa Varsovia is located off a scenic stretch of highway 34 (Costanera Sur) in the community of Villas Xihu in Esterillos Oeste on the Pacific Coast. Quepos and Manuel Antonio National Park are about an hour drive southeast on the highway. Going northwest, Playa Hermosa can be reached in 10 minutes and Jaco in 20.
